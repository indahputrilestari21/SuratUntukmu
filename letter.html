For My Future Husband

On this day, 27th March 2025, which falls on the 27th of Ramadhan, I am grateful beyond words that I have finally found you. I pray that our hearts remain guided by the same du’a, written in Lauhul Mahfudz, bringing us together in the most perfect way, at the most perfect time, by Allah’s will.

May He always protect and bless you in every step you take. May our love be filled with His mercy, and may we walk this journey hand in hand, growing stronger in faith and devotion.

Amin ya Rabbal ‘Alamin.
